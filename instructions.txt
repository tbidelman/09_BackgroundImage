**************************
BACKGROUND IMAGE CHALLENGE
**************************

Your task is to exercise your new background image skills in order to make
something that looks roughly like "photography.png"

Hints:
 - Use bg.jpg for the background image of the page.
 - Use wrap-bg.jpg for the background image of the wrap.
 - Gallery Images should be arranged in a grid with 2 rows and 3 columns.
 - Center the wrap and gallery images on the page.
